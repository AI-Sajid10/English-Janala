Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, most of our beginner lessons and practice materials are completely free for users.
3. Do I need to create an account?
Creating an account is optional, but it helps you track your learning progress and save lessons.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Practice daily with vocabulary lessons, flashcards, quizzes, and reading exercises available on our platform.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, selected courses provide certificates after successful completion of all lessons and assessments.
